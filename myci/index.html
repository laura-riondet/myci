Mycelium

The underground web of threads that links a whole forest — quietly passing food and signals from tree to tree, so nothing goes to waste and nobody goes without. It's how a neighborhood already works, once you can see it.

My city

And it's yours — my city, my street, my block. Hyperlocal by design, so the help is always a short walk away, never an app full of strangers across town.

A living network, rooted right where you live.
